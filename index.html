Over 30 Rooms… A Sneak Peek

Enter if you dare...

The Devil's Rocking Chair

From Ed & Lorraine Warren's notorious "The Devil Made Me Do It" case that inspired The Conjuring 3, this is the actual demonic throne the "Beast" claimed as its own.

Peggy the Doll

Touted as one of the most haunted dolls in the world, Peggy has caused countless nosebleeds, fainting, and even a heart attack. Guests are warned against looking directly into her eyes.

The Dybbuk Box

Known as "The Most Haunted Object in the World", responsible for multiple deaths, a stroke, and cursing famed musician Post Malone.

Dr. Death's Van

The actual death van of Dr. Jack Kevorkian, where he carried out numerous assisted suicides. A hotspot of paranormal activity, particularly for females who have seen dark shadow figures.

Demon House

The most authenticated case of demonic possession in America. Contains the actual "staircase to hell" and ritual items discovered by police buried within it. (As seen in Zak Bagans' Demon House documentary)

Bobby Mackey's Music World

The infamous haunted honky tonk from the first ever Ghost Adventures episode. Reassembled from the original building, featuring "Hell's Gate" where a demonic sacrifice unleashed pure hell.
